Short Bio

Hao Wang is a Research Associate Professor with the College of Computer Science at Sichuan University (SCU). He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU). Before joining SCU, he worked at Zhejiang Lab and was a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU). He was also a Visiting Student at the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC) for two years and a Visiting Scholar at the University of Regina.

He is also affiliated with XLearning research group at SCU.
