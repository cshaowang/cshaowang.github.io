Short Bio

Hao Wang received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU), China. He was a visiting Ph.D. Student with the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC) from 2017/9-2019/9.

He currently works as a postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of Prof. Gao Cong (NTU).
